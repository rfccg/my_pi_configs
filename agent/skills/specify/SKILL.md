---
name: specify
description: Use when starting any task, especially when requirements may be ambiguous, assumptions may diverge, or implementation can't proceed before human and AI agree on intent.
---

Interview the human to guarantee alignment on the designed task. Question relentlessly about 'what?', and 'how?' the task should be executed. Rules are:

* You may explore code base or the knowledge base to find answers and design better questions.
* Be considerate of the task complexity to regulate the number of questions. More complex -> more questions/ less complex -> less questions.
* For each question give three suggested answers and point the recommended one.
* Make one question at time. And do as many rounds as necessary.
* Questions should lead to clear definition of 'what is being built?' and 'how we are going to build?'
